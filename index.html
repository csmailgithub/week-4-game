Crystals Collector Game

You will be given random number at the beginning of the game. There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number generated at the start of the game, you lose the game if you exceed the random number. the value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Every time the game is started or restarted the value of the crystals will change.
